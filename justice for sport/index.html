At Justice for SPORT, we are committed to have the frustrations within the Sports World heard and not brushed under the rug by some legal & Advisory firms. Our comprehensive lawyer & Advisory rating platform will empower athletes to make informed decisions about future legal and advisory representation, seek justice for past injustices, and hold firms accountable for their actions. We leverage technology, media coverage, and strategic partnerships to create a fairer environment for sportspersons and disrupt the bad legal and advisory firms. Join us today and access the services you deserve together we will highlight their wrong doings and we want you to say you got Justice.
Sportspersons often face challenges in navigating the legal & Advisory landscape, with issues ranging from unfair fees to poor representation, this can be seen from several News reports such as the £1BN of bad advice report in the Guardian Newspaper. Our platform addresses these challenges by providing a centralised hub for sportspersons to rate their legal experiences and seek redress for grievances, we provide a window of information and a way to be heard for all sportspersons.
The market for legal services is vast, with sportspersons representing a significant segment of valued potential clients. However, many sportspersons struggle to find reliable information about lawyers and law firms, leading to subpar legal representation and unfair outcomes.

Our platform fills this gap by providing transparent and unbiased ratings based on real experiences. Our business revolves around the creation of a comprehensive lawyer and advisory rating platform tailored specifically to the needs of sportspersons, we look at your past and present cases, we then look at potential areas of GDPR breach, Malpractice, over billing and abuse of power by these firms and their representatives. This platform will allow sportspersons to rate firms based on various factors such as competency, professionalism, ethics, time, and fairness of fees.

Additionally, we will offer support and representation to sportspersons who have experienced unfair treatment in the past. We will take action on lawyers, advisors and the firms who have worked with high-profile sportspersons and used their image and name to attract more clients to benefit their law firm.
